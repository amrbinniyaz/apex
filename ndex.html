About Apex International School

The School With International Standard

Imagine a school where your kids are left to be kids not stifling their self-expression. The Apex International School is the first and foremost place where children can discover who they are and where their interests lie. To develop each and every innate trait of the child – the physical, the intellectual and spiritual is of more importance here. The most important part is building and maintaining an environment where kids can co-exist in harmony and freedom.

Apex International School, an English Medium (CBSE Syllabus) school is situated in Odumbra. The vision of promoters is to establish an eco friendly Higher Secondary School. We envisage a completely different school with personalized care for every child. We seek to develop intellectual and social skills and inculcate civic sense in our students. Physical well-being and hygiene are be promoted. Co-curricular activities will be given equal importance. Our school will be an extension of each family we will bring out the best in our students by working in partnership with parents. Every teacher will be a friend, philosopher and guide. We want the students to enjoy every moment of their stay at school. Our aim is to create a love for learning and to develop a sense of self esteem in the student.
